# Resources — normalising flows

Background reading for the conditioned normalising flow in this study, for anyone coming from VAEs,
GANs or GNNs but new to flows.

- **`1908.09257v4.pdf`** — Kobyzev, Prince & Brubaker, *Normalizing Flows: An Introduction and
  Review of Current Methods* (arXiv:1908.09257). A survey of the change-of-variables idea and the
  common transforms, including the spline flows used here.
- **Video** — *Normalizing Flows*, <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YPsIq_f_ihQ>. A longer, intuitive
  walk-through of the core idea.
